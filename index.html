There are an increasing number of problems in the world today ranging from social issues, environmental issues, and poverty. Whether it is lack of suitable drinking water, to conflict, to the inability of the poor to become self-sufficient, a wide variety of these problems are created by humans or simply exist as a result of our current state of societal evoltion. However, what is clear is that humans, and their brains and innovative capacity, have the abilitity to solve and tackle some of the world's most pressing issues and problems by harnessing these innovative capacities, and raising funds to realise the development of a sustainable business which resolves global issues, one at a time.
Each person in the world is either directly or indirectly impacted upon by one or more of these world problems and therefore it is in the best interest of each and every one of us to address these problems to make this world a better place for us to live in.
